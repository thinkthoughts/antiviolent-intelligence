A Constructive Heuristic Sieve for the Twin Prime Problem
Author: Yuhang Shi
Area: math.GM — Sieve Theory, Prime Density

Summary

Develops a heuristic sieve framework for approximating the twin prime constant. Rewrites sieve density products as ratios of infinite series involving symmetric polynomials of prime reciprocals, with numerical validation and analysis of truncation effects.

9423 Phase-Lock Note

Transforms multiplicative sieve products into decomposable series structures, enabling transparent analysis of density behavior. Emphasizes heuristic, constructive understanding over exact closed-form results. Parallel to constraint-based systems, where density emerges from structured filtering, contrasting with finite constraint systems that yield bounded stable densities (e.g., 24/25).
