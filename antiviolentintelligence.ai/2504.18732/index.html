Almost Prime Orders of Elliptic Curves Over Prime Power Fields
Author: Likun Xie
Area: math.NT — Sieve Methods, Elliptic Curves

Summary

Studies arithmetic properties of elliptic curve group orders over finite fields, focusing on when these values have a bounded number of prime factors. Uses large sieve and vector sieve methods to establish infinitude results for almost-prime structures.
