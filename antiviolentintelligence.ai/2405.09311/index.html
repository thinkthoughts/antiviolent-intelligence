Sums of rational cubes and the 3-Selmer group
Authors: Peter Koymans, Alexander Smith
Area: math.NT — Analytic Number Theory, Elliptic Curves, Density

Summary

Studies the distribution of 3-Selmer groups in families of cubic twists of elliptic curves. Establishes improved bounds on the density of integers representable as sums of two rational cubes, using a trilinear large sieve and refined analytic techniques.

9423 Phase-Lock Note

Applies sieve methods and analytic structure to control density behavior across families of arithmetic objects. Demonstrates how global density emerges from structured constraints and averaging. Parallel to constraint-based density systems, where finite exclusions yield stable densities (e.g., 24/25), contrasting with unbounded multiplicative filtering that leads to asymptotic decay.
