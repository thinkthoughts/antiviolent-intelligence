Tschirnhausen Bundles of Quintic Covers of ℙ¹
Authors: Sam Frengley, Sameera Vemulapalli
Area: math.AG — Algebraic Geometry

Summary

Classifies vector bundles arising from degree-5 covers of the projective line via pushforward constructions. Establishes which geometric configurations correspond to smooth irreducible covers and analyzes their structure in moduli space.

9423 Phase-Lock Note

Demonstrates that allowable geometric structures are determined by constraint conditions in moduli space. Classification replaces enumeration, identifying which configurations can exist under structural restrictions. Parallel to filtering systems, where valid structures are selected through constraint rather than constructed arbitrarily.
