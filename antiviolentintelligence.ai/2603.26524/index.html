1. Summary of the paper

Klowden and Tao discuss AI as part of a long history of mathematical and cognitive tools, emphasizing that increasingly powerful systems should remain connected to human judgment, human purposes, and real-world limits.

The central theme is that AI should function as a bounded tool that expands human thought rather than replaces it.
