Fusion

The Sun shines because hydrogen nuclei fuse into helium. Fusion releases energy long before any price symbol is written.

4H → He + energy

Stellar fusion converts mass into energy through nuclear binding. This process powers stars, drives planetary climates, and ultimately supports Earth's water cycle and biosphere.

Physical order

Fusion precedes markets, currencies, and accounting systems. The Sun existed for billions of years before any human symbol attempted to label energy with a price.

Antiviolent Intelligence begins with physical baselines: fusion, gravity, chemistry, and thermodynamics. Human systems describe these realities; they do not generate them.
