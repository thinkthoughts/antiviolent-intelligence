Surviving Eratosthenes Sieve I: Quadratic Density and Legendre's Conjecture
Author: Fred B. Holt
Area: math.NT — Sieve Methods, Density

Summary

Analyzes how structures such as prime gaps and constellations evolve under successive stages of the Eratosthenes sieve. Tracks relative populations and asymptotic behavior as filtering progresses through increasing prime constraints.

9423 Phase-Lock Note

Models survival under iterative (unbounded) sieve stages, where multiplicative filtering leads to evolving or decaying densities. Represents asymptotic sieve behavior. In contrast, finite exclusion systems (e.g., modular constraints over fixed sets) produce bounded densities (such as 24/25), where survival stabilizes rather than decays.
