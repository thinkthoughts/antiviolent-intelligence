Sifting for small split primes of an imaginary quadratic field in a given ideal class
Author: Louis M. Gaudet
Area: math.NT — Sieve Methods, Algebraic Number Theory

Summary

Applies sieve methods to detect small primes that split in an imaginary quadratic field and lie in a specified ideal class. Combines algebraic and analytic constraints to identify primes within structured arithmetic subsets.

9423 Phase-Lock Note

Applies sieve filtering under multiple arithmetic constraints, selecting primes that satisfy both field-theoretic and ideal-class conditions. Demonstrates how structured constraints define allowable subsets within larger systems. Parallel to constraint-based density systems, where finite exclusions produce stable densities (e.g., 24/25), contrasting with unbounded filtering that leads to asymptotic decay.
