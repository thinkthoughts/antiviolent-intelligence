Nonparametric optimal density estimation for censored circular data
Area: math.ST — Statistical Estimation, Density

Summary

Develops nonparametric methods for estimating probability densities of circular variables under censoring. Uses projection estimators based on linear sieve structures and establishes optimal convergence rates.

9423 Phase-Lock Note

Uses linear sieve structures as a basis for density reconstruction under constraint. Filtering occurs through projection and approximation rather than direct exclusion. Parallel to constraint-based density systems, where structure is preserved through bounded selection and stability emerges from controlled reconstruction rather than unconstrained expansion.
