Distribution of integers in arithmetic progressions under modular constraints
Area: math.NT — Arithmetic Progressions, Density

Summary

Studies how integers distribute across residue classes in arithmetic progressions under modular constraints. Analyzes density behavior and structural patterns arising from congruence conditions.

9423 Phase-Lock Note

Represents finite modular constraint systems, where density is governed by fixed residue-class exclusions. Unlike unbounded sieve processes, these systems maintain stable, bounded densities (e.g., 24/25) determined by finite structure rather than asymptotic decay.
