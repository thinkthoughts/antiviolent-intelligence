Primes in LCM recurrences: a density theorem via companion sieves
Author: Benoît Cloitre
Area: math.NT — Sieve Methods, Multiplicative Number Theory

Summary

Studies LCM-based recurrence sequences and their multiplicative increments. Introduces a companion-sieve framework to analyze prime-generating behavior and proves that the structure holds for a set of integers of asymptotic density 1.

9423 Phase-Lock Note

Uses a companion-sieve framework to establish density behavior through structured filtering across iterations. Emphasizes average (density-level) results rather than pointwise behavior. Parallel to constraint-based density systems, where global stability emerges from structured filtering rather than individual outcomes.
